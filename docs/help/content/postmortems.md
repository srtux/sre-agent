### Blameless Incident Reports
Generate a postmortem at the end of every investigation.
- **Tool**: `generate_postmortem`
- **Output**: A comprehensive report following the Google SRE template.

### Includes:
- **Title & Status**: Date, severity, and current state.
- **Timeline**: Chronological list of events.
- **Root Cause**: The "Five Whys" analysis.
- **Action Items**: Preventative measures for the future.
